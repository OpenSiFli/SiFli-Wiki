# About SiFli

## 1. Company Profile

SiFli Technologies was founded in March 2019 and is headquartered in Shanghai Zhangjiang Hi-Tech Park. It has subsidiaries and branch offices in Chongqing, Beijing, Shenzhen, and Suzhou. The team members all come from leading semiconductor design companies in the United States and China, including Marvell, Broadcom, Amazon, UNISOC, and MediaTek, among others. More than 80% of the team hold master's degrees or higher. The core team members have extensive end-to-end experience in product definition, independent R&D, and large-scale mass production. Chips developed under the leadership of these core members have shipped more than 1 billion units in total.



## 2. Product Applications

SiFli Technologies is primarily engaged in the design and application development of chips related to embedded AIoT platforms. Its products are intended for data acquisition, processing, and edge AI inference in ultra-low-power IoT, meeting the comprehensive requirements of edge computing in AIoT scenarios for computing power, energy efficiency, cost, and real-time performance. Specific applications include smart wearables, healthcare devices, smart homes, smart terminals, industrial instrumentation, and smart buildings. In less than three years, the company has completed preliminary technology research, product development and iteration, and high-volume shipments to well-known brand customers. Its products have achieved world-leading levels in both performance and power consumption.


## 3. Vision

SiFli Technologies is an innovative emerging MCU company in the AIoT era. It is committed to providing world-class MCU products and complete solutions through a new chip architecture and technological innovation in areas such as high-performance computing, ultra-low power, wireless communications, and multifunctional IoT software platforms, creating more value for society in the age of intelligence.

## 4. Open Source

SiFli Technologies is an open-source-friendly company. We actively participate in the open-source community and contribute our efforts to it. We welcome everyone to join our open-source community and work together to contribute to open source.
