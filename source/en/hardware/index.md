# Chip Hardware Design Guide

## SF32LB52x Series

Chips with suffixes 0, 3, 5, and 7 belong to the SF32LB52x series. They are powered by lithium batteries and support USB charging.

[Hardware Design Guide](/hardware/SF32LB520-3-5-7-HW-Application)&nbsp;&nbsp;
[Hardware Design Self-Checklist](https://downloads.sifli.com/hardware/files/documentation/SF32LB52%20Schematic%26PCB%20checklist_V1.0_20260121.xlsx)

## SF32LB52X Series
Chips with the suffix letters B, E, G, and J belong to the SF32LB52X Series and use a 3.3V power supply.
Chips with the suffix letter D belong to the SF32LB52X Series and use a 1.8V power supply.

[Hardware Design Guide](/hardware/SF32LB52B-E-G-J-HW-Application)&nbsp;&nbsp;
[Hardware Design Self-Checklist](https://downloads.sifli.com/hardware/files/documentation/SF32LB52%20Schematic%26PCB%20checklist_V1.0_20260121.xlsx)
