## SF32LB52 Series Boards

### SF32LB52-DevKit-LCD

LCD development board based on the SF32LB52 module. Useful for LVGL GUI development, display demonstrations, low-power Bluetooth development, audio, and wearable product prototyping.

- English guide: https://wiki.sifli.com/en/board/sf32lb52x/SF32LB52-DevKit-LCD.html
- Chinese guide: https://wiki.sifli.com/board/sf32lb52x/SF32LB52-DevKit-LCD.html
- Product family: SF32LB52x
- Key topics: LCD FPC connector, GPIO expansion, charging/discharging, audio, Impeller flashing, display expansion.

### SF32LB52-DevKit-Nano

Compact SF32LB52 chip development board for core-board use and embedded application development.

- English guide: https://wiki.sifli.com/en/board/sf32lb52x/SF32LB52-DevKit-Nano.html
- Chinese guide: https://wiki.sifli.com/board/sf32lb52x/SF32LB52-DevKit-Nano.html
- Product family: SF32LB52X 3.3 V supply version
- Design files: https://oshwhub.com/sifli/sf32lb52-devkit-nano
- Key topics: compact 21 mm x 51 mm design, castellated holes, display FPC, core-board integration.

### SF32LB52-DevKit-Core-3p3

SF32LB52 3.3 V core development board for prototyping, low-power measurement, display expansion, and hardware validation.

- English guide: https://wiki.sifli.com/en/board/sf32lb52x/SF32LB52-DevKit-Core-3p3.html
- Chinese guide: https://wiki.sifli.com/board/sf32lb52x/SF32LB52-DevKit-Core-3p3.html
- Product family: SF32LB52X 3.3 V supply version
- Key topics: 30.88 mm x 68 mm form factor, castellated through-hole header, 1.8 V/3.3 V power configuration, single-rail and whole-board power consumption tests, FPC display interface.

### SF32LB52 Huangshan Pi

Education and prototype development board with rich peripherals and examples.

- English guide: https://wiki.sifli.com/en/board/sf32lb52x/SF32LB52-%E9%BB%84%E5%B1%B1%E6%B4%BE.html
- Chinese guide: https://wiki.sifli.com/board/sf32lb52x/SF32LB52-%E9%BB%84%E5%B1%B1%E6%B4%BE.html
- External wiki: https://wiki.lckfb.com/zh-hans/hspi-sf32lb52/lckfb-hspi-sf32lb52
- Product family: SF32LB52x
- Key topics: education, prototype development, sensors, examples.

### SF32LB52-OED-6in-EPD

SF32LB52 e-paper display board and reference material.

- English guide: https://wiki.sifli.com/en/board/sf32lb52x/SF32LB52-OED-6'-EPD.html
- Chinese guide: https://wiki.sifli.com/board/sf32lb52x/SF32LB52-OED-6'-EPD.html
- Product family: SF32LB52x
- Key topics: e-paper display, FPC assembly, display interface.

### SF-DevKit-LCM-Adapter

Display adapter board for connecting supported LCD modules to SiFli development kits.

- English guide: https://wiki.sifli.com/en/board/sf32lb52x/SF-DevKit-LCM-Adapter.html
- Chinese guide: https://wiki.sifli.com/board/sf32lb52x/SF-DevKit-LCM-Adapter.html
- Key topics: LCD module adapter, FPC pin mapping, AMOLED PMIC, WLED driver, display installation.
